EDA Case Study: Business Analytics for FMCG company

 

Background: A large FMCG company has presence in PAN India. The company owns variety of brands in 3 business lines-Ice-Creams, Cakes-Biscuits and Chocolates.

The company has transaction data of its dealers (customers in this case) over the years. In this case study, data is available for the year 2014. In addition to the transaction data, the net promoter scores (NPS) are recorded for a sample of customers using online survey.

The business objective is to analyze the data and generate insights for customer behavior.

Objective: Check performance of customers in each region using measures of central tendency and variation(min,max,mean,median, coefficient of variation)

 

Calculate mean, median, maximum, minimum,SD and coefficient of variation

import numpy as np
region_per = mapped.groupby('REGION').agg(
    N=pd.NamedAgg(column="NSV", aggfunc="nunique"),
    Mean=("NSV", np.mean),
    Median=("NSV", np.median),
    Max=("NSV", np.max),
    Min=("NSV", np.min),
    SD=("NSV", np.std)
    ).reset_index()

region_per['cv'] = (region_per['SD']/region_per['Mean'])*100
