Predictive Model for Stock Movement - Case Study

Background: Machine Learning Algorithms are very useful to predict movement of stock-either to predict price or direction. In Spite of a lot of uncertainty, the well trained models can help to develop successful trading strategies. In this case study, we consider one of the leading global IT companies-Infosys and predict its next day movement using various ML algorithms.

Objective: To predict closing price direction of Infosys stock on day t+1 relative to day t .

Model 1. Binary Logistic Regression

Splitting Data into Train and Test data sets

finaldata_rm$Direction_next<-as.factor(finaldata_rm$Direction_next)

traindata<-finaldata_rm[index,]

threshold<-0.54 # based on pie chart above

model<-glm(Direction_next ~  Returns + CloseToOpen + HighToLow + Std_deviation + Returns_ndq,
