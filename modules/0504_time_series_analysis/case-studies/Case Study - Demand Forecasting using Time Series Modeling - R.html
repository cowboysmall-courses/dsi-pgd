Demand Forecasting Using Time Series Modeling: Case Study

 

Background: In manufacturing sector, many decisions are taken based on forecasting future demand. The demand forecasting helps companies to manage accurate, timely and effective production. The sports goods manufacturing company identified 3 top products for which demand forecasting was most critical. The 5-years monthly sales data for 3 products was used to develop the model.

Objective: Forecast next 3 months sales using trend and seasonality exhibited over 5 years of sales data.
